FAQ

A shared direction, made clear.

How is an initiative different from a project?

Projects in the Hub showcase individual or team work. Initiatives are NexCore Labs-led community efforts with an ongoing purpose, a lifecycle, and public accountability.

Can students support an initiative?

Yes. Follow NexCore updates to hear about new launches, contribution opportunities, and ways to share feedback with the team.

Will campaigns and events appear here?

Yes. Ongoing platforms, focused programs, campaigns, and events can all be listed with a clear type and status.

Why are some initiatives archived?

Archive status keeps useful context visible while clearly showing that an initiative is no longer active.
