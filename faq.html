Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NexCore Labs, our services, and how to get the most out of our platform.

General

What is NexCore Labs?

NexCore Labs is an independent, student-led platform built to empower the SQU community to do more through useful tools, shared knowledge, project visibility, collaboration, and practical digital support. It is not operated, sponsored, endorsed, or licensed by the university unless NexCore publishes written authorization stating otherwise.

Who can use NexCore Labs?

NexCore Labs is open to students at universities and colleges across Oman, including research teams, student startups, clubs, and creative projects. Eligible SQU accounts currently receive free platform access under NexCore's own access rules. Prices for other users remain visible for transparency, but paid orders are temporarily paused.

When was NexCore Labs founded?

NexCore Labs was founded on September 23, 2025 by SQU students who wanted to help their community access useful tools, share work, collaborate, and move ideas forward.

How do I get started with NexCore Labs?

Sign in with your Google account. Eligible SQU accounts and already approved users can access their dashboards under NexCore's current access rules. Other users may review the Pricing page, but new paid orders are temporarily paused during legal and regulatory review.

What makes NexCore Labs different?

NexCore Labs is broader than a web development service. It is an independent, student-led platform focused on helping the SQU community discover tools, present work, collaborate, and turn ideas into practical outcomes.

Is NexCore Labs website Progressive Web App (PWA) compatible?

Yes! NexCore Labs is built as Progressive Web Apps. This means users can install the site on their devices like a native app, enjoy offline functionality, and get push notifications. It's a modern, user-friendly approach that boosts engagement.

How is performance optimized?

Pricing

How is pricing structured?

NexCore uses a flexible "build your own" model — no rigid plans. You start with Lifetime Access (0.950 OMR), which is always included, then optionally add Priority Review (0.300 OMR) and choose a support duration. You only pay for what you actually need. All prices are in Omani Rial (OMR).

What does Lifetime Access include?

Lifetime Access (0.950 OMR) has no scheduled renewal fee and continues for the operating life of the relevant NexCore service. It does not promise that NexCore or a third-party hosting provider will operate forever. See the Pricing & Billing Policy.

What support options are available?

Support covers Email & WhatsApp-based technical guidance for your NexCore project. Options: No Support (Free), 1 Week (0.250 OMR), 1 Month (0.500 OMR), 3 Months (1.000 OMR), 6 Months (1.500 OMR), or 1 Year (2.500 OMR).

What happens after the support period ends?

Your access is not tied to the optional support period. When support ends, you can purchase another fixed-duration support package or contact us for separately scoped work.

Can I get a refund?

You may request a refund within 7 calendar days only before fulfillment begins. A customer may not request a voluntary refund after the project is published, access is activated or used, setup or priority review starts, or the customer benefits from any paid feature. NexCore service failures remain eligible for a full refund or another agreed remedy.

What payment methods do you accept?

Paid service orders are temporarily paused. PayPal checkout for paid services, bank transfer, mobile transfer, and paid order submission are unavailable while NexCore completes its legal and regulatory review. Please do not send payment for a service using old details or links.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contract or automatic renewal. Access is a one-time charge, and optional support is purchased for a fixed period.
